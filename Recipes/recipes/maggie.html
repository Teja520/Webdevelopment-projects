Maggie recipe

Description

Maggi noodles is essentially made of wheat flour, edible vegetable oil, salt, wheat gluten, mineral (Calcium carbonate) and Guar gum. The instant noodles packet also contains a sachet of masala TASTEMAKER inside which is to be added to the noodles after preparation in boiling water

Ingredients

  • one maggie packet
  • half a glass water

Steps

  1. Take one and a half cup of water in a pan.
  2. Heat the pan on medium flame
  3. When the water comes to boil, add the Noodles Cake to the pan.
  4. Heat the pan on medium flame
  5. When the water comes to boil, add the Noodles Cake to the pan.
  6. Cover it with a lid for a minute
  7. After a minute, uncover the lid and add the tastemaker to the pan
  8. Mix it well.
  9. Without breaking the Noodles.
  10. Switch off the flame when all the Water boils.
  11. Enjoy the hot Maggi in this lovely weather
